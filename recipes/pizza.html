Pizza Recipe

Description & Recipe

Now this pepporoni pizza is made with only the finest ingredients. This particular recipe is from an owner who knows how to make the best pizzas on the planet. Here's a little more info on this particular pie.

Made with tomatoes sources from the hills of southern California, this pizza is made for you. This recipe is easy to learn. Some pizza recipes are overly complicated and they tend to be hit or miss. When you come across a pepporoni pizza recipe the main ingredient you want to look for is simplicity. Don't get me wrong, there are pizzas that require more care but this pie is best with simple ingredients and concise steps. We will take a look at some snazzier recipes later on.

Ingredients

  • Pepporni
  • Cheese(mozzarella)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Dough
  • Parsely

Steps:

  1. Take the dough and knead it per your best dough making know-how. Make sure it is not overly kneaded. Spead into circle leave at 1/2 inch thickness with crust portion rolled
  2. Prepare the tomato sauce in heated stovetop.
  3. Evenly distribute sauce over the dough until it is spread to the ends leaving the crust bare.
  4. Place in 350 degree preheated oven and bake for 15 minutes.
